Here is more information about me as a professional.



I attended Morehead State for my undergrad.

I finished with a 4.0 in Elementary Education and Special Education.

I started teaching at Lawrence County High School using my special education degree.

I worked there one year.
I then worked at Blaine Elementary School for 6 years.

While at Blaine Elementary I completed a Master's Degree from Western Governors University. My degree there was in Curriculum and Instruction.
I then went to work at Louisa East Elementary. I have been there for 7 years now.

Since I've been at Louisa East I have completed a second Master's Degree from Morehead State as well. This degree was in Curriculum and Instruction. This gave me my Rank 1.

I also got my National Board Certification in Literacy.

I decided to go back to school to get my Library Certificate in hopes of being the next librarian at Louisa East.
